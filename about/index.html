About

I am a recent college graduate with experience kickstarting and managing global projects in education, urbanism, trade, and public policy.

After working in European Parliament and for the Executive Office of the President, volunteering on four continents for an international NGO, conducting research in a dozen countries and founding a handful of grassroots community development initiatives, I received a Fulbright Scholarship to coordinate educational programs and implement public diplomacy initiatives in Southeast Asia.

I am recognized by my managers and peers as a leader, designer, innovator and lover of efficiency. I am experienced in program design, conflict resolution, event planning, leadership development, communication strategy, stakeholder affairs, grassroots advocacy and research.

I enjoy 35mm photography, experimenting in the kitchen and learning about flags
